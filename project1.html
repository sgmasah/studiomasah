Welcome to the future of Eilandje, Antwerp. Tucked away on the former Rijnkaai Scheldekaaien parking lot, this cutting-edge project serves as a model for revolutionary city living and a lighthouse of urban renewal. This concept adds a modern touch while retaining the classic elegance of famous Flemish courtyard designs, which are recognized for their exact floor layouts and well-kept hedges. Consider using structured hedges to create a labyrinthine area that is both dynamic and engaging, rather than just as decorative elements. This is not your average courtyard; rather, it's a vibrant center of activity that offers chances for people of all ages, setting a new standard for urban development in the future.

This concept is more than just architecture; it is a dedication to community-centric, walkable living areas that foster wellness and connection. It is inspired by the health-centric ethos of the world's blue zones. Here, we develop homes in order to promote a feeling of community and belonging, rather than merely building dwellings. Come along on this innovative architectural and community-transforming trip where every nook and cranny is intended for more than just habitation.
