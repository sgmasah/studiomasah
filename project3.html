Welcome to the future of Gouda Oost—a community-driven transformation redefining what a library can be. More than just a place for books, this project reimagines a neighborhood hub where learning, gathering, and creativity come together. Designed as an open and inviting ‘living room’ for the community, this space fosters connection and ownership, allowing residents to shape its evolving identity. Built with transparency and warmth, the design embraces a restrained aesthetic, using natural materials like timber and polycarbonate to enhance tactility and openness.

The massive timber framework acts as both structure and invitation—an adaptable canvas where the community takes center stage. Active ground-floor spaces, both indoor and outdoor, encourage spontaneous encounters, while the flexible layout supports a range of communal activities, from workshops to neighborhood events. This is more than a library—it’s a catalyst for social interaction, lifelong learning, and shared experiences. A place where architecture serves people first, inviting them to inhabit, adapt, and grow alongside it. Step inside and discover a space that belongs to everyone.
