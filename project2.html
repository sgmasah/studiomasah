Welcome to the future of El Garrote, Tigre—a transformative project redefining urban living for elderly retirees in Buenos Aires. Our sustainable redevelopment integrates contemporary design with neighborhood charm, creating vibrant, safe, and inclusive spaces. Starting with new infrastructure on empty plots, our phased strategy ensures residents transition seamlessly to new homes without displacement. Buildings, ranging from four to six stories, feature adaptable designs based on a 3x3 grid, fostering a sense of community.

Active ground floors with cafes and shops enhance safety and social interaction, turning streets into dynamic hubs. This project goes beyond construction, promoting belonging and diversity through a mix of social and middle-income housing. Join us in reimagining El Garrote as a beacon of sustainable, community-centric living. This is more than just a redevelopment project; it is a commitment to creating a thriving, inclusive neighborhood for elderly retirees. Experience a place where every detail is designed for comfort, connection, and a better quality of life.
