Welcome to the future of El Garrote, Tigre, a neighborhood-led transformation that reimagines urban living through sustainable, inclusive design. This redevelopment focuses on creating dignified, high-quality social housing that enhances everyday life for current residents. By working within the fabric of the community, the project introduces safe, vibrant homes and public spaces while preserving the area's identity. A phased development strategy allows families to transition gradually into improved housing, ensuring minimal disruption and fostering long-term belonging.

Built on a flexible 3x3m grid, the housing typology encourages social connection through shared courtyards, adaptable layouts, and active ground floors with cafes, small shops, and communal services. Streets are designed to be lively and walkable, promoting interaction and safety. This initiative is not just about new buildings, it’s about investing in people, strengthening local networks, and creating a future where everyone has access to comfortable, well-designed homes within a connected and resilient community.
